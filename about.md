Hi, I'm **José Carlos Andreu Galán** — a security engineer based in
Pontevedra. I work in mobile and application security at
[Applus+ Laboratories](https://www.applus.com), evaluating how apps and
systems hold up under attack. Before moving into security I spent years as
a mobile developer (iOS and Android), and that builder's instinct still
shapes how I look at things.

This blog is my notebook for **technology, science, the internet and the
curiosities** that catch my attention along the way — from security and
software to whatever rabbit hole I've fallen into lately. It's minimalist
and built for reading, in the spirit of the classic
[microsiervos.com](https://www.microsiervos.com).

I'm also an **amateur radio enthusiast** — you can find me on the air with
the callsign **EA3HZE**.

I write [**The Side Channel**](https://side-channel.com), a
newsletter with curated reading on cybersecurity, hacking and the net.
You'll find more of my work on [GitHub](https://github.com/joscandreu),
and you can reach me at [joscandreu@gmail.com](mailto:joscandreu@gmail.com).

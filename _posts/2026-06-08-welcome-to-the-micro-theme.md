**Micro** is a minimalist Jekyll theme inspired by the classic blog
[microsiervos.com](https://www.microsiervos.com). It is designed to run
directly on **GitHub Pages**, with no forbidden plugins or tricks that
would break the automatic deployment.

<!--more-->

## What's included

- A two-column layout with a sidebar, collapsing to one column on mobile.
- Archive pages by **section** (category).
- Client-side **search**, with no server.
- Pagination on the home page.
- Automatic SEO, sitemap and RSS feed.

## Getting started

Edit `_config.yml` with your title, description and social links. Then
create posts in the `_posts/` folder. That's it!
